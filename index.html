The Yunnan snub-nosed monkey is a type of golden monkey. In the 40 years since the Baima Snow Mountain Nature Reserve was established in Yunnan province, this black-and-white monkey has been saved from extinction, and its population has risen to more than 3,800.

SNUB-NOSED SPECIES

There are five species. With the exception of the Myanmar and Tonkin monkeys, these species are only found in China.
